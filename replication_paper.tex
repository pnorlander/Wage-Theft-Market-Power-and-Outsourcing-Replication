\section{Replication Notes}
%===============================================================================

\subsection{Data and Sample}

The analysis uses administrative data on H-1B visa petitions merged with Wage and Hour Division enforcement records. The main estimation sample consists of 1,237,750 observations at the firm-occupation-commuting zone-year level for firms with at least one violation during the sample period.

\subsection{Key Variables}

\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{logviol}: $\log\left(1 + 1000 \times \frac{\text{Violations}_{foct}}{\text{H1Bs}_{foct}}\right)$
\item \texttt{logbw}: $\log\left(1 + 1000 \times \frac{\text{Back Wages}_{foct}}{\text{H1Bs}_{foct}}\right)$
\item \texttt{logbwpw}: $\log\left(1 + 1000 \times \frac{\text{Back Wages}_{foct}}{\text{Wages Promised}_{foct}}\right)$
\item \texttt{HHI}: Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (three-year rolling average), where 1 = monopsony and 0 = perfect competition
\item \texttt{SUB}: Binary indicator for subcontractor firms (1) vs.\ lead firms (0)
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Software Requirements}

\begin{itemize}
\item Stata 16 or higher
\item Required packages: \texttt{estout}, \texttt{reghdfe}, \texttt{ppmlhdfe}, \texttt{nwcommands}, \texttt{ivreg2}, \texttt{xtivreg2}
\item \LaTeX\ distribution for PDF compilation
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Running the Replication}

Execute the master do-file in Stata:
\begin{verbatim}
do code/00_master.do
\end{verbatim}

This will generate all tables in \texttt{output/tables/} and figures in \texttt{output/figures/}, then compile this document.
